Comprehensive Motor Insurance

The most complete coverage that protects against both third-party liabilities and damage to your own vehicle.

Key Features:

  • Covers damage to your vehicle from accidents, natural calamities, and theft
  • Includes third-party liability coverage
  • Personal accident cover for owner-driver
  • Option to add riders like zero depreciation, engine protection
  • Cashless repairs at network garages
  • 24/7 roadside assistance available

Third Party Motor Insurance

Mandatory coverage that protects against legal liabilities to third parties.

Key Features:

  • Covers injury/death of third party
  • Covers damage to third party property
  • Mandatory as per Motor Vehicles Act
  • Lower premium than comprehensive policy
  • Does not cover damage to your own vehicle
